About Company

Tailorman started out in 2013 with a simple aim to change how men perceived fashion in India. We brought together traditional Indian craftsmanship and cutting-edge technology to create a suit that embarks unparalleled fit and quality. START TO FINISH As one of the biggest manufacturers in the world, we pride ourselves in owning the entire process from design and manufacturing to retailing our premium garments.

Origin

At Tailorman we are all about celebrating the Individual, the little nuances that make you; YOU! Our love for perfection extends beyond the realms of men’s clothing and extends to lifestyle, architecture, sports, travel and the individual themselves. In the pursuit of excellence we strive for bring forward a unique take on a MAN’S WORLD through inspiring stories, trending news and style hacks. Our Tailorman section on the blog talks about the latest collaborations, collections and updates on our in-house brand Tailorman.
